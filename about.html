Informations about the site

This site is a web tool for flexible visualization of global terrorism data. It provides statistics and visualizations in various formats, including cartographic representations by regions. The system supports multi-criteria search for reported incidents, with additional details on weapons used sourced externally.

Easily export your findings in a variety of formats, including CSV for detailed analysis, PNG for quick snapshots, and SVG for scalable visualizations. Seamlessly navigate through a multitude of incidents using our advanced multi-criteria search feature, delving deep into incident reports enriched with supplementary information on weapon types sourced from external datasets.
