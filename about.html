Information about the site

Welcome to our platform dedicated to providing flexible data visualization of global terrorism trends. Our mission is to empower users with comprehensive insights into terrorist incidents worldwide, facilitated by our custom REST API. We understand the importance of accessible data analysis and that's why we offer a range of visualization methods, including statistical representations and interactive maps segmented by regions.

But we don't stop there. Our platform enables seamless exportation of data in CSV, PNG, and SVG formats, allowing users to further analyze and share insights effortlessly. What sets us apart is our commitment to providing multi-criteria search functionality. Users can delve deep into incident reports, exploring details such as weapon usage, sourced from external data repositories.

Driven by a passion for data transparency, we continuously enrich our resources to enhance data visualization capabilities. Join us in unraveling the complexities of global terrorism, one visualization at a time.
